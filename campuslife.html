Campus Life

Eskişehir Technical University offers its students a rich campus life in addition to academic success. You can watch our promotional video about our university below.

🏋️ Sports Facilities

The campus features a fully equipped gymnasium, an indoor sports hall, outdoor soccer and basketball courts, and a running track. All students can use these facilities at affordable prices.

🎭 Cultural Activities

ESTÜ hosts a variety of cultural events throughout the academic year, including theatre plays, music concerts, art exhibitions, film screenings, and technology fairs. The main cultural center and conference hall can accommodate over 1000 guests.

🍽️ Dining & Social Areas

Our campuses have their own cafeterias where students can eat two meals a day at affordable prices. Additionally, our campuses have cafes and social facilities where students can socialize. Every year, our school organizes a spring festival during the spring semester.

🌳 Green Campus

ESTÜ is committed to sustainability. The campus boasts extensive green spaces, bike paths, and ongoing projects focused on renewable energy and waste reduction. Students are encouraged to participate in the Green Campus Initiative.
