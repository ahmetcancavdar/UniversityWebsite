🎓 Scholarships & Student Support

ESTÜ students can benefit from a wide range of financial support opportunities including government grants (YÖK, TÜBİTAK), university-funded scholarships, and externally sourced bursaries coordinated through the Career Development and Student Support Unit. Part-time work positions on campus are also available. For application details and deadlines, contact the Student Affairs Department.

🏥 Health & Psychological Counseling

The ESTÜ Health, Culture and Sports Department provides primary healthcare services to all registered students free of charge. The campus health center is open Monday–Friday, 08:00–17:00. In addition, professional psychological counseling services are available for students who need personal, academic, or emotional support. Appointments can be made in person or via the student portal.

🍽️ Dining Services

ESTÜ operates a campus-wide dining system with multiple cafeterias serving affordable, balanced meals throughout the academic year. Students can track daily menus, load balance, and manage meal card transactions through the dedicated Yemekhane Portal (Dining Portal) available on the university's digital platform.

💻 Academic Advising & Digital Systems

Each student is assigned a faculty advisor upon enrollment for academic guidance. ESTÜ provides a fully integrated digital ecosystem for student operations:

  • ÖGRBİLSİS — Student Information System for course registration, transcripts and enrollment
  • OYS — Learning Management System for course materials and assignments
  • Institutional E-mail — @eskisehir.edu.tr address provided to all students
  • Eduroam — Secure campus-wide Wi-Fi accessible with university credentials

🔧 Student Clubs & Project Teams

ESTÜ hosts a vibrant student community with over 80 active clubs spanning technology, arts, sports, culture, and social responsibility. The university also has an official Student Project Teams Coordination Office, supporting interdisciplinary teams that compete in national and international competitions in fields such as robotics, aerospace, software, and sustainable engineering. Students are encouraged to join or establish new clubs via the Student Union Office.

🎯 Orientation & Campus Life

First-year students at ESTÜ benefit from a structured orientation program coordinated by the Orientation Coordination Office. Mandatory orientation courses ESTÜ101 and ESTÜ104 introduce new students to university systems, academic expectations, and campus resources. Cultural and artistic events, career fairs, and social activities organized throughout the year contribute to a vibrant and inclusive campus life.

🌍 International Mobility

ESTÜ actively participates in the Erasmus+, Mevlana, and Farabi exchange programs. Every year, hundreds of students and academics benefit from international mobility opportunities for studies, traineeships, and research. Incoming and outgoing exchange applications are coordinated by the International Relations Office. For program details, eligibility, and application periods, visit the International Relations Office or the student portal.
